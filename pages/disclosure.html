  5. Finance Options
    1. In order to assist you with obtaining vehicle finance, your details will be passed over to finance providers with whom we work with.
    2. Our finance providers, in order to assess your creditworthiness, will check your information with credit reference agencies, and potentially fraud prevention agencies.
    3. They will also use the information that you have provided us to assist them in assessing your current status and affordability.
    4. It is important that all the information you provide is accurate and up to date.
    5. Please note that we cannot guarantee an acceptance and all potential finance is subject to terms and status.
    6. Before entering into any agreement, you should ensure you have fully read and understood the agreement you are considering entering into.
    7. We encourage you to take the time to read through any documentation provided, and please do ask any questions you may have.
    8. It is incredibly important you understand the type of agreement you are entering into along with your rights under the agreement such as (but not limited too) Distance Selling and the 14-day rule, Post Contract and your rights if there is an issue with the vehicle, Cancellation and what would happen should you not be able to keep up with the repayments.
    9. Our team is happy to answer any questions or go through anything in further detail, please contact us on the above.
    10. We will never provide you with a recommendation or advise you on certain products, however our partners will provide you with all the information required within your Pre-Contract Information to assist you in making an informed decision.
  6. Affordability
    1. It is extremely important that you look into finance options that are suitable for your current (and potential future) financial situation.
    2. We will go over all your details to ensure we have the correct information to pass onto our partners, who will then try to verify the information. However, please note that evidence of your income may be requested.
    3. If you are aware or suspect that your financial position will change in the future, you must inform us or the finance partner directly.
    4. Your credit rating could be adversely affected if you do not make payments when due.
