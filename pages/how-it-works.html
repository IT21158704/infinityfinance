How it works...

Once approved, we tell you the maximum you qualify for, also known as a "credit limit."
We will answer any questions you have and provide any help or support in your search for the vehicle you want to buy.

If you need a higher credit limit, just call us, and we will look at the options available. We usually send a
selection of vehicles to you to get you started, and you can use the filters in the list to choose your
ideal one. You can buy from any reputable dealer. If you need help finding a specific vehicle, just let us
know, and we will make a no-obligation search and provide you with a selection of options. If you know what
you want and have found the vehicle you want to buy, just call or email us with the details.

Once you have chosen a vehicle you want to buy, we'll provide a quote and email the documents for you to
e-sign once we have checked the vehicle for you and you're happy to proceed. You don't need to visit an
office; everything is completed over email and telephone. The dealership you're buying the vehicle from is
paid directly.

Once the dealer is in receipt of the funds, you'll drive the vehicle
away. The process is simple, quick, and usually takes no more than 48 hours.

As a matter of course, we organize a check on the vehicle for you to ensure there's no accident or hidden
problems with the vehicle. We also value the vehicle to ensure that you're not paying over the odds for it,
and we will advise you if there are any issues.
