Infinity have been helping customers since 2007 we could help you

Getting car finance at Infinity Finance means we offer you a wide range of finance options, when you submit a finance application, we’ll seek out the lowest rate finance package available to you. We offer access to our best panel of lenders to ensure better rates regardless of your credit history. We pride ourselves in offering a fast and efficient Service. Once you have been approved for car finance, we work quickly to get you the funds you need without any hassle, stress or fuss. You can then choose your vehicle from any reputable UK dealership and drive your vehicle away within a few hours.

Our car finance products are based on your overall credit profile, not just on your previous financial history. We have a large panel of lenders who can help people with all types of credit profiles. The finance deal offered will be subject to your individual credit status and circumstances.

If you have a Bad Credit History, you have come to the right place

If you have a bad credit history, this can be caused by many unfortunate events like losing a job, falling ill, a relationship breakdown or a failed business. It can consist of things as simple as a late payment on a mobile phone or a utility bill but also more serious things, like CCJ’s, Defaults, Arrears, Bankruptcy or IVA’s, Unfortunately, once you have a bad credit rating it will be registered on your credit file for at least 6 years

For you it can be difficult to be approved by normal high street lenders once they have any of these issues registered against the credit file. You will be deemed riskier, as you have a less than perfect track record compared to people who have never experienced any financial difficulty. The good news is at Infinity Finance, we have many specialist lenders who are used to helping people in these circumstances.

It is important to understand, that if you have very minor issues, you will be offered better rates than people with more severe credit issues. Each application is looked at individually and it is not a case of one rate for everybody. We will offer you the best deal from our panel of lenders.
